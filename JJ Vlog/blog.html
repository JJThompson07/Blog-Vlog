A Lads Night Out

Opening up your group chat on Whatsapp to a friends post

"Anyone fancy a couple of drinks at the Local?"

Instantly followed with a few of the other lads in the group agreeing to the pub and starting to arrange a time.
This, however, is not an innocent invitation to the pub. Every Lad knows that the pub will only be the start of the night, once a few cold ones have gone down, someone will pop up on their round and decide that shots or Jager Bombs are needed.
Everyone now knows that town is ivenitable, it was always inevitable!

Might Aswell be Posting this and asking who wants to join
Table, Bottles and no girls in sight

Taxis arrive outside the pub, everyone finishes theirs drinks and make there way to the taxis.

"Anyone booked a table?"

Another phrase commonly used for the guys who like to splash their cash to try to attract the girls.
Turning up to the club and pulling a promoter to the side, one of us manages to sort a table, we then all walk straight through and right to the table. Vip bands are then thrown around whilst a few of the guys try to sort the drink for the table, handing the money over as if it was their own and not a share of everyone together.
Now the night really begins, the drinks arrive, everyone goes to the dancefloor to do their own thing returning to the table to keep themselves topped up and regroup.

standard night photo

The next morning, everyone wakes up and looks at the whatsapp group where one of the lads has written:

"Story time boys..."

This is now where the best news of the night is shared, and the lads who cant remember what they were doing, find out the antics they got up to.
Most of the time the stories tend to focus on the lad that really over cooked it - "dick of the night" - and everyone rips into them.

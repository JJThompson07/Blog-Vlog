About me

I am 22 years old from the East Midlands in the UK.
This blog site was created as a project to practice my coding but also to provide a schedule and routine for myself.
Like most people I like to spend my free time with my mates on nights out, days out or holidays together.

I like to keep myself active a try to play as many sports as I can, mainly football as I play about 4 or 5 times a week. Some people tell me that I am too competitive, however, I believe that competition is healthy, it helps to improve the experience and also I would not see the point in taking part if the aim was not to win.
My aim will be to share activities I get up to that I find interesting and worth sharing, this could be nights out, sports, events or day treks with friends to somewhere.
I hope to try keep this entertaining enough to read and enjoy what I upload to this site.
